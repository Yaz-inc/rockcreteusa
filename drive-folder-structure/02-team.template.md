# 02 - Team

Personal artifacts for the team working on Rockcrete USA Website Rebuild.

## What goes here

- CVs (in primary and secondary languages if bilingual).
- Government IDs / passports / cédulas (if required by the client or funder).
- Signed NDAs (per teammate).
- Tax / payment forms (W-9, W-8BEN, etc.).
- Extended professional profiles or LinkedIn exports.
- Headshots for client-facing materials.

## What does NOT go here

- Contracts the team signed → `01 - Procurement and Contracts/`.
- Client team members' CVs → `03 - Client Documents/` if they shared them.

## Suggested subfolders

```
02 - Team/
├── CVs (en)/
│   ├── Project-Lead-Daniel Gonell.docx
│   └── ...
├── CVs (en)/   (if bilingual)

│   └── ...

    └── Extended bios for proposals.
```

## Naming

Use ASCII hyphens:

- `Project-Lead-{{LAST_NAME}}.docx`
- `Project-Lead-{{LAST_NAME}}-Passport.pdf`
- `Project-Lead-{{LAST_NAME}}-NDA-rockcreteusa.pdf`

## Privacy and retention

This folder contains personally identifiable information. Restrict Drive sharing to the team. After the engagement closes:

- Consult with each teammate about retention preferences.
- Move IDs to `99 - Archive/` and lock further sharing.
- Keep CVs and profiles indefinitely, since they support the consultant's portfolio.

## CV hygiene

Before circulating CVs externally, verify each has:

- Phone and email contact block.
- Current location.
- Languages.
- Academic credentials section.
- Most-relevant prior work for this engagement, prioritized.

If anything is missing, fix in the working draft and re-upload here. Track gaps in `docs/project-memory/status.md`.
