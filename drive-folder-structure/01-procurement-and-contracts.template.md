# 01 - Procurement and Contracts

Active and historical commercial paperwork for Rockcrete USA Website Rebuild.

## What goes here

- Active SOWs and signed contracts.
- Change orders.
- Statement-of-work amendments.
- Letters of agreement.
- Procurement dossiers (proposals, justifications, scoring sheets).
- Insurance certificates, NDAs, MSAs.
- Historical proposals (the ones that lost, didn't ship, or were superseded). Keep them under `Historical Proposals/` so they don't clutter the active set.

## What does NOT go here

- CVs and personal IDs of consultants → `02 - Team/`.
- Client-supplied reference material → `03 - Client Documents/`.
- Draft proposals that may still ship → keep them in your active drafts, not in this folder yet.

## Suggested subfolders

Adapt to your engagement's structure:

```

├── Active/

    └── (anything that didn't make it into the active engagement)
```

## Naming

Use ASCII hyphens to separate the doc type, the project/client, and the date. Examples:

- `SOW-rockcreteusa-website-rebuild-2026-05-06.docx`
- `Change-Order-001-rockcreteusa-website-rebuild.docx`
- `Amendment-002-rockcreteusa-website-rebuild.docx`

Spaces and accents are fine in Drive but the filename should still be unambiguous and sortable.

## Privacy

Anything in this folder may be sensitive. Restrict Drive sharing to the team. **Never** commit this folder's contents to Git, even if Git is private; contracts often have confidentiality clauses that prohibit version-control distribution.
