# Interview invitation template

Three flavors for three audiences. Pick the one closest to the stakeholder's tier, edit, send.

All three follow the same shape: warm intro, specific ask, time / format / prep ask, confirmation request, sign-off.

## A. Executive / Tier-1 stakeholder (60 to 90 minutes)

Subject line:

```
Rockcrete USA Website Rebuild interview: a conversation on {{TOPIC}}
```

Body:

```
Hi {{STAKEHOLDER_NAME}},

I'm Daniel Gonell, leading Rockcrete USA Website Rebuild for Rockcrete USA. {{CLIENT_PRIMARY_CONTACT}} suggested I reach out.

I'd love to schedule a 60-to-90-minute conversation to understand:
- {{TOPIC_1}}
- {{TOPIC_2}}
- {{TOPIC_3}}

Your perspective is essential because {{WHY_THIS_PERSON}}.

I can work around your calendar. A few options that work for me:
- {{SLOT_1}}
- {{SLOT_2}}
- {{SLOT_3}}

(Or send me a few that work for you and I'll grab one.)

Format: I'll come prepared with structured questions but expect to follow your lead. I'll send the question list 24 hours ahead so you can scan it.

Working language: en (also comfortable in en if you prefer).

Recording: I'd like to record for synthesis; the recording stays with the consultant team and isn't shared outside the engagement without your permission.

Thank you for considering. I'll send a calendar invite once we lock a slot.

Best,
Daniel Gonell
info@newmindsgroup.com · {{PROJECT_LEAD_PHONE}}
Live engagement context: https://rockcreteusa.projectizer.ai/
```

## After sending

1. Update `stakeholder-register.md` "Confirmation status" table with the slot proposed.
2. When the slot is confirmed, update again.
3. After the interview, use `meeting-notes-template.md` to capture, save under `research/interviews/YYYY-MM-DD-{slug}.md`, and update the register once more.

## Cadence guidance

- Tier 1 stakeholders: contact directly, ideally with an intro from someone who knows them.
- Tier 2: cadence flexible; schedule when their content is needed for an upcoming deliverable.
- Tier 3: batch invitations; group similar roles together to share findings.
- External / funder: limit to one or two interviews per engagement unless the funder requests more.

## Privacy

- Save the actual sent emails in `01 - Procurement and Contracts/Sent/` in Drive, not in Git, because email content sometimes contains personal info that should not be in a versioned repo.
- The interview transcripts go in Git under `research/interviews/`, redacted if necessary per the privacy section in `docs/project-memory/source-of-truth.md`.
